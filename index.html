Word Evolution

Word Evolution is an evolution simulator. The user inputs a short phrase and the app responds with a dozen random text strings. These strings are then run through an algorithm that determines which of them are the most similar to the user's input. The strings that are most similar are allowed to "survive" while the others are deleted.

The surviving strings are then put through an "interbreeding" algorithm, and their "children" inherit their properties. Over generations, the end result is a short phrase that is an identical match of the user input.
